About ACB3P2

The ACB3P2 database is a curated repository of antimicrobial peptides (AMPs) with demonstrated anticancer activity that are capable of crossing the blood-brain barrier (BBB). These peptides represent a promising class of therapeutics for the treatment of brain cancers, particularly glioblastoma and brain metastases.

Key Features:

  • Comprehensive peptide information including sequence, structure, and activity data
  • Detailed anticancer activity profiles with IC50 values against various cancer cell lines
  • Blood-brain barrier penetration data from in vitro, in silico, and in vivo models
  • Antimicrobial activity profiles
  • Toxicity and stability information
  • Links to primary literature (PMID, DOI)

Applications:

  • Drug discovery and development for brain cancers
  • Structure-activity relationship studies
  • Computational modeling and prediction
  • Comparative analysis of peptide therapeutics
